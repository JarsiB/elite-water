ABOUT FILTRATION PLANT

Water Filtration Plant (WFP)

Raw water contains undesirable sediments, colour, algae (which can produce taste and odour), and other harmful organisms. A Water Filtration Plant (WFP) is designed to remove these impurities and produce water that is safe and suitable for drinking.

Water treatment is any process that improves the quality of water to make it appropriate for a specific end-use such as drinking, industrial supply, irrigation, recreation, or safe discharge back into the environment.

Filtration plays a crucial role in protecting human health and ensures water is fit for both domestic and industrial applications.
