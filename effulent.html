EFFLUENT TREATMENT PLANT

What is an ETP?

  • ETP (Effluent Treatment Plant) is a process designed to treat industrial wastewater for reuse or safe disposal into the environment.
  • Influent: Untreated industrial wastewater.
  • Effluent: Treated industrial wastewater.
  • Sludge: Solid matter separated from wastewater during the treatment process.
